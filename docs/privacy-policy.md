# HomeFinder Privacy Policy (MVP)

**Last updated:** 2026-04-14

## 1. Scope
This policy explains how HomeFinder MVP collects and processes user data for core flows: search, save, contact, and account access.

## 2. Data collected
- Account data: full name, email, phone.
- Interaction data: search filters, listing views, save actions, contact actions.
- Technical data: request identifiers, device/app telemetry, crash/error logs.

## 3. Purpose of processing
- Provide core product functionality.
- Improve reliability, fraud/spam prevention, and security monitoring.
- Measure product KPIs (search-detail-save-contact funnel).

## 4. Legal basis
- Contractual necessity for account and transaction-like actions.
- Legitimate interest for reliability/security analytics.
- Consent where required by local regulation.

## 5. Retention
- Contact requests and operational logs are retained per internal retention policy.
- Data is deleted or anonymized when no longer required for service/legal purposes.

## 6. Data sharing
- Data is shared only with required service providers for hosting, analytics, and monitoring.
- No sale of personal data.

## 7. Security controls
- Token storage encryption at rest in app layer.
- Request tracing (`X-Request-Id`) and endpoint idempotency keys for critical writes.
- Access control and least-privilege operational access.

## 8. User rights
- Request access, correction, deletion, or export of personal data.
- Contact support: `privacy@homefinder.example.com`.

## 9. Policy updates
Policy updates are versioned and announced in release notes.
